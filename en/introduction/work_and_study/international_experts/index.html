Support for foreign professionals in the health sector in the Czech Republic

Support for foreign professionals in the health sector in the Czech Republic EURAXESS Services Network was established in 2004 and so far has involved more than 40 (mostly European) countries. Collaborating contact points in the Czech Republic were created in 2005-2007. Currently, EURAXESS Czech Republic network consists of two service centers (only Prague and Brno) and seven local contact points.

EURAXESS Czech Republic offers foreign scientific researchers and their employers information and services related to their planned stay in the Czech Republic. It deals mainly with:

  • Encouraging the mobility of scientists and researchers and their family members
  • Providing information, consultancy and assistance services to foreign R & D staff
  • Providing support to employers of staff members
  • Improving the social integration of foreign R & D staff and their family members into Czech society
  • Removing barriers in international scientific mobility
  • Searching for suitable staff, corresponding to specific requirements, through the portal EURAXESS Jobs
  • EURAXESS Czech Republic Web sites : http://www.euraxess.cz/
  • EURAXESS Jobs: http://ec.europa.eu/euraxess/index.cfm/jobs/
