Useful Links

  • The Institute of the Czech Language of the Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic - http://www.ujc.cas.cz/

    The Academy of Sciences of the Czech Republic is the main public institution dealïng with elementary research in the Czech Republic. The Institute of the Czech Language belongs to the section of humanities and philological sciences. It was established in 1946 from the original Office of the Dictionary of Czech Language that existed since 1911. It deals with research concerning contemporary Czech and its history.
