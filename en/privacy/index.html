Protection of Personal Data

A warm welcome to mluvtecesky.net and thank you for using our website! We attach great importance to protecting your personal information and would like to inform you here about the form and scope of the collection, processing and use of personal data in connection with the use of our site.

Your personal information will be treated confidentially in accordance with the relevant data protection regulations laid out in the German Federal Data Protection Act (Bundesdatenschutzgesetz) and in this privacy statement. It will neither be published nor passed on to third parties.

We would like to make you aware that data transmission over the internet (e.g. when communicating by e-mail) can lead to security loopholes. Complete protection of data against access by third parties is not possible.

The contact details published due to the requirement to provide publishing information may not be used by third parties to send unsolicited advertising and information materials. We reserve the right to legal action in the case of the unsolicited distribution of promotional information, such as spam e-mails.

Information collected by us

1.1. Personal data

Some of our services can be used without registration on the website. However, to be able to make use of all the features of the website, you must create an account.

In order for our services to function, we need to collect certain personal information during registration and process it. This Information consists of the following data:

  • Username
  • E-mail address
  • Password

Your e-mail address and password are never visible to other users. We will not disclose this data to third parties and / or provide it to them in any other way.

The following user registry data are always fully visible to other registered users:

  • Username
  • Date of registration on the website
  • Date of last login
  • User language

Some other information about you may be stored, for example, if you enter additional data in your personal profile, or if you communicate with the administrators of the website. Providing this information happens purely on a voluntary basis.

The following personal data will be visible to other users, if you enter them, in addition to the required information, in your personal profile:

  • Name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Country
  • Information about me
  • Photo
  • Links to social network profiles

You can deactivate the publishing of these additional data at any time here https://mluvtecesky.net/user/settings.

This website contains various learning materials: courses, exercises, word lists, tests, a library etc. Data about the linguistic progress of registered users, for example relating to lessons already completed, vocabulary, completed tests, etc. are collected and stored on the web server. The purpose of this data collection is to help users learning German by, for example, showing the learning materials in the correct order to give advice about further learning or displaying the linguistic progress of each user on the website. Some of this data may be visible to other users, for example in the form of accumulated points.

As a registered user you can send private messages to other users. These private messages between users are stored on the servers of the website and are under no circumstances made ​​available to other users. The messages are automatically deleted once they have been deleted from mailboxes by all the participants. Public messages in discussion forums are stored permanently, unless they are deleted by the users who posted them, or by webmasters. This storage carries on after a cancellation of the Service. Other users can see the extent to which you engaged in activities in the discussion forum.

We send users e-mails for various reasons such as registration validation, information about newly received messages, etc. E-mail messages sent by the employees of this website are stored. E-mails that are considered to be business letters are not deleted during the statutory retention period. The system of the website also records e-mail addresses to which an e-mail could not be delivered in order to request an update of the e-mail address. In addition to this, e-mails that are sent to users may contain graphics that when opened, record whether an email has been opened. This is done anonymously and without attribution to a person or a user. The same applies if you click on a link in an e-mail from mluvtecesky.net. Our Messages may also involve information about our services (newsletter). You can revoke your consent for us to use your e-mail address to send the newsletter at any time https://mluvtecesky.net/user/settings. Each newsletter from mluvtecesky.net contains a unique link that may be used to discontinue receipt of further newsletters.

1.2. Log data

When you visit our website and every time you call up a file, our web server collects log information on these processes (e.g. browser type, date and time of the call). These are not personal data. Thus, we are not able to assign the recorded data to a specific person.

All of this information is used by us purely for statistical purposes and for the detection of defective links or program errors. Disclosure to third parties will not happen. These data are not combined with other data by us. The data are deleted regularly, within a week after a statistical analysis.

1.3. Cookies

Cookies are small files that are placed by the website visited, using the browser, on a user's computer to enable the server to recall information about the user at a later date.

This website employs two types of cookies:

  • Temporary cookies: Session-cookies are used to facilitate navigation on the site. These cookies store a randomly generated, unique identification number, called a session ID, and expire automatically after the end of the session.
  • Permanent cookies: They make it possible to store your encrypted username and password locally on your computer. With the "Remember me" function, you can save this cookie permanently to allow automatic signing in. This cookie will expire automatically after 30 days, but can also be cleared by logging out.

Both session cookies and permanent cookies are used to store user preferences such as language choices, voice recognition settings and user IDs in order to simplify use of the website for you.

You can turn off the use of cookies either specifically for this site, or in general to all websites, using the settings on your Internet browser. More information can be found on the website of that internet browser. When disabling cookies, however, some functions, such as automatic logins to websites, can no longer be used as intended.

2. Using social networks
